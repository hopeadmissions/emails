At their very best and most beautiful, sports bring people together. Here at Hope, we know this full well. While we offer championship athletics and transformative experiences on our own campus, with SEED (Sport Evangelism to Equip Disciples), some of our student-athletes choose to use their passion for sports to transform others (and themselves!) by sharing the love of Christ internationally.

Since the summer of 2017, SEED has sent 75 Hope student-athletes, plus 13 coaches and administrators, on seven trips to five countries (Costa Rica, Ghana, India, Uganda and Zambia). Their mission: To radiate hope, faith and love by conducting sports clinics and making clean water accessible.
