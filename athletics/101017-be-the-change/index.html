At their very best and most beautiful, sports can bring people across countries and cultures together. Here at Hope, we know this full well. While we offer championship athletics and transformative experiences on our own campus, with SEED (Sport Evangelism to Equip Disciples), some Hope College student-athletes choose to use their passion for sports to transform others (and themselves!) by sharing the love of Christ around the world.

Here is what our Hope student-athletes say about their two-week international SEED trips:

“This trip to Zambia allowed me to see that although we come from different backgrounds, we still worship the same God.”

“It’s true that we blessed people in Costa Rica, but in the end, we found that the blessings came mostly from them.”

“Going to India was a reminder that we should carry God’s principles with us each day and love those around us unconditionally.”

Read more about SEED on our blog. Watch a short video from the summer. View photo galleries from Costa Rica, India and Zambia.

For a world in need, hope (and Hope) makes a difference. We’d love to tell you more about how you can make a difference through sport, too. Plan your visit to Hope today!

Be Strong. Be True.
