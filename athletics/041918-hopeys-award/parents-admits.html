Last night at our eighth annual Hopeys Awards, our Hope athletics community came together for an evening of celebrating a year’s worth of accomplishments. Created in the style of the Espys, the Hopeys recognize the best of Hope athletics as we honor the most exceptional team, the best role player, the breakthrough athlete and the most outstanding male and female athletes, to name a few.

While all Hopeys award winners surely earn and deserve their honors — engraved and presented in an emblematic wooden shoe — every Hope team and every Hope student-athlete are truly winners, too. You see, what we believe the Hopeys are about more than anything else is celebrating the camaraderie and unity of our Hope student-athletes, coaches, staff, and community. It’s a fun and festive night to honor hard work and teamwork achieved by all who wear orange and blue.
