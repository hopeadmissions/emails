Borrowing to pay for a college education is a wise investment. Many Hope students take out loans, so you are not alone. In fact, our students report back after graduation that they can meet their loan commitments and their Hope experience is one of the best investments they have made in their future.

Students who have been awarded a Federal Direct Loan and/or a Federal Perkins Loan as part of their financial aid package must complete entrance counseling and promissory note requirements as soon as possible. You can view your financial aid requirements on your KnowHope Plus account. Visit the Office of Financial Aid website to complete these requirements if you haven’t done so already.

Considering additional loan options?

Federal Parent PLUS Loans are an option for families who have submitted a FAFSA (financial aid application) for the 2015-2016 school year. Private student loans are available to families who are looking for additional financing opportunities. These loans are optional and are available to US residents only.

  • explore our private student loan comparison tool
