All of the residence halls at Hope College have their own laundry facilities. The washers and dryers use pre-paid laundry cards.

We really encourage you to save an extra step and avoid lines during Orientation Weekend by ordering your pre-paid laundry card now! If you order it soon, your card will be available at your residence hall when you move in.

Are you an athlete? You might need to do laundry after that first day of practice! Be ready to throw that load of wash in right away. If you order your laundry card now, it will be ready at the Student Development office (DeWitt Center, first floor) when you get here.
