Application and Scholarship Processing
  • All applicants have a "portal" through which they can see whether their application is complete. Information about this portal is sent to the student's email address after receiving the application.
  • Students whose applications remain incomplete are sent an email (to the student's email address) letting them know and reminding them of the portal as a way to see what is missing.
  • Students whose applications are complete by November 1 are sent a decision letter the first week of December. Applications completed after November 1 are processed on a rolling basis. These applicants are usually notified within 4-5 weeks after the application becomes complete.
  • Students eligible to receive an academic scholarship are sent a letter about 3 weeks after the letter of admission.
