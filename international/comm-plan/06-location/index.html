Hope is proud to be situated just one block away from the thriving downtown of Holland, Michigan. Sitting on the shore of beautiful Lake Michigan, the Holland community is often described as friendly and welcoming. But don’t take our word for it:

  • All American City — National Civic League
  • Top five safest cities — Forbes Magazine
  • #1 Safest and Secure mid-sized city — Farmers Insurance
  • Second happiest and healthiest — USA Today, Gallup Poll
  • America’s Prettiest Towns — Forbes

Some more fun facts:

  • With an area population of about 115,000, Holland is small enough to feel comfortable and safe, but large enough to have lots of resources and events.
  • Because of our distinct and beautiful seasons, Holland is a major tourist attraction year-round.
  • Just 30 minutes from Grand Rapids (Michigan's largest thriving city), world-class events are often easier to attend than living right in a major metropolitan area.
  • Holland is also a quick three-hour train ride from downtown Chicago, Illinois.
