What is the Phelps Scholars Program?

The Phelps Scholars Program is a unique residential program at Hope College that combines academic coursework with social and community activities that emphasize cross-cultural understanding to prepare first-year students to be global leaders. Students in the program live together in community in Scott Hall.

In conjunction with their coursework, Phelps Scholars are partnered to serve with local organizations in our community such as the Boys and Girls Club, Holland Hospital, and Latin Americans United for Progress. But it’s not all about the work!

Phelps Scholars enjoy weekly activities in Scott Hall, and attend several field trips throughout the year to places like Chicago, the Detroit-area and Indianapolis, to participate in cultural experiences.

Recognized by the Association of American Colleges & Universities as an exemplary program in higher education, Phelp Scholars students represent a broad range of backgrounds and perspectives.

We encourage you to check us out when thinking about your first year of college. The Phelps Scholars community invites all students to consider living and learning with us next year.
