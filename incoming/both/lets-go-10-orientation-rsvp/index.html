Hello Hope Parents and Families!

We are looking forward to your participation in Hope College Orientation Weekend. Recently, your student received a booklet that outlines the weekend's activities. In case you have not seen the booklet, ask your student and please check out our website.

During Orientation Weekend, there will be sessions that acquaint you with important aspects of the college so that you will leave knowing more about what your student will experience here. You will have opportunities to meet other parents and to talk with Hope faculty and staff. Additionally, on Saturday at 4 pm, you are invited to join your student for their First Year Seminar or Transfer Student Session!

We need each student to RSVP for Orientation. Family members joining us for the weekend should be listed on the form. This will ensure that there are nametags ready for you when you arrive. These nametags are your ticket into the Dining Hall for the weekend!

If your student is living in a residence hall, there will be a welcome bag and orientation packet waiting for you at check-in. If your student is commuting or lives in a cottage or apartment, the welcome bag and orientation packet will be at the Student Life Desk located on the first floor of the DeWitt Center.

Please let us know about any questions or concerns. We are happy to help!

Go Hope!
