Odds & Ends

As you prepare for your arrival on campus in a few short weeks, here are some details you may be wondering about. We hope the items below address any remaining questions that you may have. Should you have any additional questions, please do not hesitate to contact the appropriate office here at Hope. We look forward to having you on campus!

Parking Permits

Students who would like to have a car on campus, must register their vehicle with Campus Safety. This registration includes a registration fee. Annual parking fees are $300, reduced to $150 for those entering at semester. Once purchased, these permits must be picked up at the Campus Safety office and properly displayed to be considered valid.

Laundry Cards

All of the residence halls at Hope have their own laundry facilities, The washers and dryers use pre-paid laundry cards. You may pre-purchase a laundry card now and pick up in the Student Development Office when you arrive on campus. If you have any questions, do not hesitate to contact Student Development, sdev@hope.edu, 616.395.7800.

Textbooks

The Hope-Geneva Bookstore serves Hope Students by providing the textbooks, reference books, materials and supplies that are required or recommended for course use. If you would like to see a list of books for a course, compare prices and place online orders, you can check the online booklists. Typically, booklists are posted two to three weeks before a term begins. However, the spring semester may be available only one week prior to classes beginning due to small preparation time between semesters and the holidays. Keep checking!

Dining

Want to learn more about your dining options for when you arrive on campus? Hope College Dining Services offers a wide variety of dining options and is continually seeking to provide new offerings that will provide Hope students with both food that they enjoy along with a great dining experience. Also, Hope College Dining Services is a major employer on campus, learn now to apply on our web site!
