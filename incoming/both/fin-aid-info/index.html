Students who have been awarded a Federal Direct Loan and/or a Federal Perkins Loan as part of their financial aid package must complete entrance counseling and promissory note requirements as soon as possible. You can view your financial aid requirements on your plus.hope.edu account. Visit the Office of Financial Aid website to complete these requirements if you haven’t done so already.

Considering additional loan options?

Federal Parent PLUS Loans are an option for families who have submitted a FAFSA (financial aid application) for the 2016-2017 school year. Private student loans are available to families who are looking for additional financing opportunities. These loans are optional and are available to US residents only.
