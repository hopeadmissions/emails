PAYING YOUR HOPE COLLEGE BILL

${Contacts.Preferred First Name},

Your first Hope College bill is available for you to review through your plus.hope.edu account once you have registered for classes. You may be interested in having your parents or other individuals view and make payments.

Here's what you need to know about our e-bill system:

  • All bills are sent via email and can be paid online or by mailed check.
  • Bills are automatically sent to enrolled students. You have the option to set up "authorized users" to view and make payments on your student account.
  • The installment plan option is available ($25 fee per semester). An auto-payment option is also available (no additional fee).

Setting up authorized users:

  • Log in to plus.hope.edu to access your student account.
  • Click on Registrar and Student Accounts > Student Accounts > Online Bill and Payment
  • In the Parent PINs section, create a username and enter an email address for each parent or other individual who you would like to be able to view your bill.
  • These authorized users will be given a temporary password. After resetting their password, they can view bills, make payments and enroll in the installment plan.

For those enrolled in the installment plan, the first payment is due December 31, 2016.

If you do not enroll in the installment plan, payment in full is due January 9, 2017.

Questions about bills, student accounts, payment plans and setting up authorized users? Please contact Business Services.
