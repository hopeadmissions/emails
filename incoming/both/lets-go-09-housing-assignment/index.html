Hope College Housing & Roommate Assignment

  1. Visit plus.hope.edu.
  2. Enter your User ID and PIN. Click Login.
  3. Click Housing and Roommate Info.
  4. Select a term (Fall 2016) and click Submit.
  5. View your housing assignment and mailing address.
  6. View your roommate(s) and their contact information.
  7. Exit when you are finished.
