Welcome to Hope College!

On-campus housing is more than four walls, a bed, and finding creative ways to store your clothes and books. At Hope, we take great care to match students with roommates in order to foster a sense of community in our residence areas. The first step in that process begins with you completing your housing application.

Tell us about yourself! Are you super organized or on the messier side? Night owl or early bird? Do you like to study in complete silence or with music in the background? All of these factors (and more!) help us to make the best arrangements for your living experience in the spring.

Please complete your application by December 21.

To plan ahead, move-in is scheduled for noon on Saturday, January 7, 2017. Check in will be at the Student Development Office, located on the first floor of the DeWitt Center.

Cynthia Vogelzang, Housing Coordinator
