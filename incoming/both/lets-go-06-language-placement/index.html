We strongly recommend taking your language requirement during your first year at Hope College:

  • Better retention of what you have already learned in high school.
  • Gain up to sixteen (16) Hope College credits for lower level courses taken right away if you have placed into a higher level language class.
  • Study abroad opportunities can happen more quickly by completing these language credits sooner. Studying abroad isn't just for language majors — it's a great experience for those majoring in economics and business, history, fine arts, and more.

If you have never studied a foreign language or wish to begin studying a new language, you may enroll in any beginning language course for Fall 2016! Choose from Arabic, Chinese, Dutch, French, German, Greek, Japanese, Latin, Russian and Spanish.
