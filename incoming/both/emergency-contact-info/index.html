${Contacts.Preferred First Name},

The safety of every person at Hope College is important. Your cell phone number has been registered to receive emergency notifications from the HOPE ALERT system.

HOPE ALERT messages are distributed in very specific circumstances. They may be sent for severe weather, class cancellation, or campus threat or emergency requiring your immediate attention.

HOPE ALERT messages are not sent to advertise events or solicit donations. Your cell phone information will not be shared with other parties. To update or remove your cell phone record, please visit plus.hope.edu. Click Personal Information > Update Emergency Contacts. In this area you may also enter the information for someone (parent, family member) to be contacted in the event of an emergency.
