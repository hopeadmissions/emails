There are a number of ways that we encourage a healthy lifestyle at Hope College. As a new student, you will hear more about physical activity, food choices, sleep habits and taking care of yourself during your first months on campus.

We also need to keep track of your health history and health insurance information. (Don't have health insurance? We have an option for that, too! See Step 5 below.) Please complete the steps below by June 10, 2016. Quick tip: Scan or take a picture of your immunization record and health insurance card (both sides) before you get started.

  1. Visit the Hope College Health Center Patient Portal.
  2. Log in with your 1HOPE (Hope College email) username and password. Remember to keep this username and password private.
  3. Forms tab: Complete the health history form, TB questionnaire and insurance verification for new students.
  4. Immunization tab: Enter the dates of your past immunizations.
  5. Insurance tab: Enter your health insurance information. If you do not have health insurance, please visit our site to purchase health insurance via UnitedHealthcare.
  6. Upload tab: Upload copies of your immunization record, insurance card (both sides) and TB skin results (if required).
