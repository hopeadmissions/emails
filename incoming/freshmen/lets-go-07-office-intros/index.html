Student Development

  • Plans events and activities, including Orientation
  • Coordinates housing assignments
  • Offers counseling and psychological services
  • Addresses student conduct concerns
  • Provides career development resources
  • sdev@hope.edu, 616.395.7800

Business Services

  • Prepares charges and credits to students’ accounts
  • Processes payments
  • Assists with payment plans
  • Distributes bill notifications
  • busofc@hope.edu, 616.395.7810

Office of the Registrar

  • Creates class schedules
  • Coordinates academic advising and registration
  • Maintains educational records
  • Tracks graduation requirements
  • Reports grades and processes transcripts
  • registrar@hope.edu, 616.395.7760

Health Center

  • Treats acute and chronic illnesses
  • Performs physicals and women’s annual exams
  • Addresses mental health concerns
  • Provides health testing and immunizations
  • Assesses injuries
  • healthcenter@hope.edu, 616.395.7585
