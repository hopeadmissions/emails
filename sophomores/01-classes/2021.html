Dear ${Contacts.Preferred First Name},

As a sophomore, you may be starting to think about where you’ll apply to college, but more likely you’re trying to figure out which classes to take next year! We’re here to help.

It’s helpful to know what we’re looking for as we review applications for admission to Hope. In general we hope to see these kinds of classes in your high school curriculum:

  • 4 years of English
  • 4 years of social studies
  • 3 years of math
  • 3 years of lab science (chemistry, biology, etc.)
  • 1-2 years of foreign language (two years is preferred)

Your junior year will likely be one of your most comprehensive (and challenging!). And while it may be tempting to pump the brakes and take a lighter senior year — don’t do it! We’ve found there is a direct correlation between the strength of your senior year course schedule and your success in the transition to college.

As always, we’re here to help! Please reach out to your admissions rep with any questions you might have.

Sincerely,

Bill Vanderbilt
