Your new home at Hope.

Student housing. At Hope College, it’s more than four walls and finding creative ways to store your clothes and books. We take great care to match students with roommates to create a sense of community in our residence areas. The first step in that process starts now!

Tell us about yourself! Are you super organized or on the messier side? Night owl or early bird? Do you like to study in complete silence or with music in the background? All of these factors (and more!) help us to make the best arrangements for your living experience in the fall.

Housing applications are due on Monday, June 8, 2015. Some of you may have completed this during Admitted Student Day. If you haven’t completed it yet, please find a few minutes in your busy schedule to complete this short form.
