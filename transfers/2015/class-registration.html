We look forward to your arrival on campus for the Fall 2015 semester. As a transfer student, we will be working with you individually to create a personalized class schedule that will help you toward the fulfillment of your intended major and general education requirements.

Beginning the registration process:

  1. With your transfer credit in mind, review the general education curriculum and requirements for any major/minor you are considering.
  2. Explore the Class Schedule to find courses that interest you for the fall semester. Course descriptions can also be found in the College Catalog.
  3. Ask lots of questions. We’re happy to help!
  4. Contact us to schedule an in-person meeting to register. Or email us your course selections. Online registration is not available for your first semester at Hope. Transfer student registration begins Tuesday, May 26.

Viewing your transfer credit:

Log into KnowHope Plus to view any credits we have transferred from your previous school and/or AP and IB exams.

Helpful Tips

  • First-Year Seminars are not required for transfer students.
  • A full-time course load is 12-16 credits; most students take 14-16 credits each semester. There is an additional fee for each credit over 16.
  • Find several alternate class options. We may not be able to add you to a closed class.
  • You’ll work with us to register for your first semester at Hope. Your assigned academic advisor in your intended major field that will help you register for future semesters.
