Recently, Hope’s hometown of Holland, Michigan, was named the #2 “Happiest, Healthiest Place to Live in America.” Are we happy to live in Holland? You bet!

Hope College is a four-season kind of campus, and we couldn’t be happier about that. We love living and learning in a place where each season brings its own beauty and fun.

  • Fall: You can still catch sunny days at the beach before the weather gets crisp and the leaves start changing. (Plus, apples, cider and sweatshirt weather! Need we say more?)
  • Winter: Snow means sledding, snowshoeing and skiing. And with Holland’s underground snowmelt system, the streets and sidewalks of downtown are clear for walking and jogging and shopping, too.
  • Spring: Campus blooms in April! Plus, nearly 5 million tulips — planted for the city’s annual Tulip Time & Tulipanes festivals — bring downtown Holland to life.
  • Summer: Ah summer! Sunsets along our sandy, picturesque Lake Michigan coastline, an 8-minute drive from campus, are spectacular!

No matter what time of the year, our award-winning downtown — just a block from Hope’s campus — is alive with cafes, restaurants, shops and theaters.

Plan your visit to Hope College today. We can’t wait to show you around our campus and our hometown!
