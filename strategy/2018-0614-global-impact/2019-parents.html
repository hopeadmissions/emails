Chicago resident and Hope grad Leecox Omollo ’02 believes strongly in the importance of global connections and a broad worldview. Coffee, he’s found, is one way to work toward those important purposes. Thanks to his Hope education, Leecox says he was well prepared to start the Kikwetu Kenya Coffee Company in 2014, a venture that made his life goals a reality.

Leecox came to Hope from Nairobi, seeking an education in computer science. He credits Hope for equipping him well with skills and knowledge in his chosen field, but his West Michigan experience is what started his love for coffee, and he and his wife, Martha, plunged into entrepreneurship. That love for coffee now connects people and elevates communities all over the world.
