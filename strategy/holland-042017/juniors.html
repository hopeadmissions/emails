Holland, Michigan, is described many ways: Friendly college town. Beautiful beach getaway. Relaxing vacation destination. Picturesque community. And for more than 3,200 Hope students: Home.

Our hometown is a pretty special place:

  • Forbes Magazine named Holland one of “America’s Prettiest Towns” and “Top Five Safest Cities in the U.S.”
  • The Gallup-Healthways Well-Being Index ranked Holland #2 as the “Happiest, Healthiest Place to Live in America”
  • WalletHub rated Holland the #1 small city in the nation to start a business — a great benefit to our entrepreneurial students and alumni

Holland will explode with color in a few weeks as nearly 5 million tulips bloom across town. Even in the winter, when an underground snowmelt system keeps downtown sidewalks dry, the city is inviting. With the award-winning downtown just a block from Hope’s campus, students are within walking distance to cafes, shops, theaters and recreation. And, the sandy beaches of Lake Michigan are just an 8-minute drive away.

I invite you to visit Hope College this summer. While you’re here, explore all that Holland has to offer.
