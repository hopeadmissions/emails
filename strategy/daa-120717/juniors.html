At Hope, the college experience is much more than attending class and taking exams. On our campus, you’ll enjoy the best of arts and culture, with opportunities that inspire creativity and expand horizons, no matter your major.

Love being in the audience? Our campus calendar is filled with entertaining programs and events — music, dance and theatre performances, films at “The Knick,” workshops by writers, visits by poets, and more.

Eager to be in the spotlight? You can share the stage or studio with other dedicated musicians, actors, dancers, artists and writers. Throughout the year, our students participate in Musical Showcase, H2 and StrikeTime dance companies, OPUS magazine, art exhibits, and theatre productions.

If you’re considering a major or minor in visual arts, creative writing, dance, music, or theatre, you may be eligible for a Distinguished Artist Award — a renewable, annual $3,000 scholarship for students with strong academic records and outstanding creative abilities.
