Dear ${Contacts.Preferred First Name},

At Hope, life outside the classroom is as vibrant as it is inside. Whatever your passion, whatever your interest, we’ve got you covered with more than 50 student-led organizations.

  • Mad for your major? Explore and apply your discipline in Engineers Without Borders, Pre-Law Club and award-winning Club Animalia.
  • Serious about R+R? Find quiet relaxation in Yoga Club or an adrenaline rush in Outdoor Adventure Club.
  • Champion for a cause? Check out Green Hope, Colleges Against Cancer and Hope United for Justice.
  • Eager to expand worldviews? Gain and share new perspectives through Black Student Union, Latino Student Organization, Hope’s Asian Perspective Association and Theta Gamma Pi.
  • Wanting to worship? Make a joyful noise with Sacred Dance, Intervarsity Christian Fellowship or Hope Catholics.
  • Interested but inexperienced? Discover skills you never knew you had in Hope College Motor Sports, Quidditch and Ultimate Frisbee.
  • Competitive in spirit? Go head-to-head against competitors from other schools in Sailing, Rugby and Hockey Club Sports.
  • A future journalist? Find your voice with student media groups for The Anchor, WTHS and Opus.
  • An aspiring leader? Develop leadership, responsibility and character through Student Congress, Student Activities Council, and eight fraternities and eight sororities.

If clubs aren’t your thing, no worries — you’ll still enjoy being part of our dynamic community where all are invited for fun and friendship. Get your calendar ready for improv and comedy shows, slam poetry, concerts by national acts, films in the Pine Grove, and distinctive Hope traditions like The Pull, Nykerk and Spring Fling.

Sincerely,
Richard Frost
Vice President for Student Development and Dean of Students
