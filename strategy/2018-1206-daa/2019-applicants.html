Want to share the stage or studio to showcase your talents with other dedicated musicians, actors, dancers, and artists? Shape your path in graphic design or creative writing, too? Study at Hope College where you’ll do all of this while learning from professors committed to your artistic growth and success.

With nationally accredited programs in dance, theatre, music and visual arts, Hope College has a long-standing tradition of providing outstanding instruction and experience in every major arts area. Our students hone their crafts by participating in Musical Showcase, H2 and StrikeTime dance companies, OPUS magazine, art gallery exhibits, Hope Summer Repertory Theatre, Visiting Writers Series and much more.

We’ve invested more than $50 million into our newest arts facilities, the Kruizenga Art Museum and the Jack H. Miller Center for the Musical Arts. Combined with the DePree Art Center and Gallery, DeWitt Theatre and the Knickerbocker Theatre, we have exceptional spaces for you, too.

Apply for a Distinguished Artist Award (DAA) in:

  • Visual Arts
  • Creative Writing
  • Dance
  • Music
  • Theatre

Each DAA grants a $3,000 scholarship annually (renewable for up to four years) to students with strong academic records and outstanding creative abilities.

Whether you plan to major or minor in the arts, there are countless opportunities to flourish at Hope. We know you’ll find your place here to stand out and shine!
