Dear ${Contacts.Preferred First Name},

At Hope, academics are rigorous. Inside the classroom and out, our faculty and staff will challenge you to pursue new and exciting goals. And, they will support you and cheer you on — sometimes in surprising ways.

Take Hope’s “Study Break Breakfast.” This late-night tradition, held the Monday evening of finals week, gives students the opportunity to relax and recharge over “Brinner” (Breakfast+Dinner=Brinner)… and who doesn’t appreciate a little maple syrup and pancakes at 10:00 p.m.?

Your servers for this event will be staff and administrators from Student Development, who understand how a little encouragement — and a little fun — can make all the difference when the pressures of final exams hit. It’s just our way of saying, “We’ll help nourish your body and spirit as you work hard to nourish your brain.”
