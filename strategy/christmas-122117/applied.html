Dear ${Contacts.Preferred First Name},

Christmas is in the air here at Hope College. Wreaths and garland adorn the archway outside of Graves Hall. Festive snowmen stand in front of residence halls. Dining hall menus feature holiday-themed meals. With the last of their final exams complete, our students bid farewell to one another as they head off for the break.

This is a season of both beauty and busyness, but above all, it is a season of great promise. We hear this promise in the angel’s message to the shepherds:

Do not be afraid. I bring you good news of great joy that will be for all the people. Today in the town of David a Savior has been born to you; he is Christ the Lord. This will be a sign to you: You will find a baby wrapped in cloths and lying in a manger.

Let us, like the trembling shepherds, be reminded of God’s wondrous promise and comforted by His Love for all people. As we celebrate the humble birth of the Prince of Peace, may our hearts be filled with hope for all that is yet to come.

Thank you for considering Hope as your next educational home. From our Hope home to yours, Merry Christmas!

Spera in Deo,
