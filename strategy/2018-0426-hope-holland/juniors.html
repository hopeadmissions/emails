Holland, Michigan, is described in many ways: Friendly college town. Beautiful beach get-away. Picturesque community. And for more than 3,200 Hope students: Home.

Hope’s hometown is a pretty special place with its small-town charm and big-city amenities. An award-winning downtown is just a block from campus, so Hope students are within walking distance to cafes, shops, theaters and recreation. We’re especially excited about a new downtown development opening in early 2019 that will include an 11-screen cinema and restaurants.

In the spring, Holland erupts with color as nearly 5 million tulips bloom. Even in the winter, when an underground snowmelt system keeps downtown sidewalks dry, Holland is inviting. And anytime of the year, the shores of Lake Michigan are just a 10-minute drive from our campus.

We hope you’ll consider calling Hope and Holland “home.” Visit soon to see our wonderful community.
