Holland, Michigan, is described in many ways: Friendly college town. Beautiful beach get-away. Picturesque community. And for more than 3,200 Hope students: Home.

Hope’s hometown is a special place with its small-town charm and big-city appeal. The city attracts millions of visitors from around the world all year long. For Hope students, award-winning downtown Holland is just a block away from campus, which means cafes, shops, theaters, recreation and more are all within walking distance.

In the spring, Holland erupts with color as nearly 5 million tulips bloom during the city’s annual Tulip Time festival. In the winter, an underground snowmelt system keeps downtown sidewalks dry, inviting shoppers, joggers and walkers to stay active even in the snowiest months. And, with Lake Michigan just 10-minutes away, local beaches and hiking trails invite students to get outdoors all year ‘round.

This summer, we invite you to visit Hope in Holland for one of our Anchor Days (more information coming soon!). Or, schedule your individual visit for any weekday this summer.
