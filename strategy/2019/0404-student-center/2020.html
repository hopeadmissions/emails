The new Bultman Student Center, located in the heart of campus, opened in fall 2017 after years of planning and anticipation from our campus community.

Today, this new 42,000 square-foot facility is home to several offices and resources that serve our students including Student Life, The Center for Diversity and Inclusion, Student Development, the Registrar’s Office and Counseling and Psychological Services.

It’s also the place where students meet up for social activities like open-mic and movie nights, group meetings, study time, coffee breaks and quick meals from the Kletz Market.
