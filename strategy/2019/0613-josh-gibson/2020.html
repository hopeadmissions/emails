“Last year, when Josh won the Nicklaus Award and some of us were there to witness his receiving the award from Jack Nicklaus himself, we commented that this was a ‘once in a lifetime’ experience,” Lokers said. “Well, now we know that with Josh, it is actually a ‘twice in a lifetime’ experience. What an incredible honor for Josh and his family. I am so happy for him.” —Scott Lokers, head coach of Hope College Men’s Golf
