At Hope College, you’ll receive an undergraduate education that includes graduate-level opportunities. Our students collaborate with their professors on research, scholarly work and creative performance at the highest level. So, you won’t just sit and listen to your professors; you will work alongside them.

  • Traveled to China to study economic policies and business development.
  • Confronted an invasive species in West Michigan’s hemlock forests.
  • Helped oncologists to assess the best medicines for certain patients.
  • Discovered how people find meaning in suffering.

  • Designed theatre costumes for form and function using historical and literary context.
  • Formed a working partnership with The American Library in Paris.
  • Made a breakthrough discovery toward better medicine for Parkinson’s disease.
  • Wrote a software program for the benefit of Hope nursing students.
  • Researched how our diets affect our sleep patterns.
