When you enroll as a Hope student, we welcome you into your new community. We hope that you find a place to live where you can meet new people, hang out with friends, study, chill as well as sleep — your own "Home Sweet Hope.”

Students at Hope live on campus for their first three years, and many opt to live on campus for their final year.

Take a tour of our first-year residence halls →

As you learn, develop and grow in and outside of the classroom, your Hope housing options grow with you. You’ll start college living in communal residence halls your first and second years, then move to more independent but college-supported cottages and apartment living during your junior and senior years.

In addition to this list, many first-year students combine residential life, academic engagement, and social events by joining unique living-learning communities such as the Phelps Scholars and Day 1 programs. Juniors and seniors have the option to join the Emmaus Scholars living-learning community.

You’ll find these and other Hope housing options as places where you can anchor yourself throughout your college journey. Apply today to live and learn at Hope.
