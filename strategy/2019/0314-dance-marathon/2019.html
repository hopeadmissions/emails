Hope College has a heart for serving others and this past weekend was the perfect example of that. The 20th annual 24-hour Dance Marathon was held on campus with a record-number 1,100 students dancing all night and day to raise a record-setting $340,172.20 on behalf of the children served by Helen DeVos Children’s Hospital of Grand Rapids, MI. And, the event was organized entirely by Hope students. Wow!

In the 20 years from 2000 through 2019, Hope’s Dance Marathon raised a total of more than $2.67 million. Dance Marathon is just one example of Hope students giving back and living out Hope’s mission to lead lives of leadership and service. We invite you to join us at Dance Marathon next year, and the first step is to complete your Common Application. You can make a difference here, too!
