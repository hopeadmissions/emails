Dear ${Contacts.Preferred First Name},

Last fall, the new Bultman Student Center opened to much anticipation in the heart of our campus. Students who were once in your shoes planning their entry into college are now using this beautiful facility at every opportunity.

You, too, can enjoy all this 42,000 square-foot student-space has to offer at Hope: a living-room-like lounge featuring a fireplace and expansive views of our Pine Grove, a cafe with study spaces, a prayer chapel, a theater and a large open activity room for events.

The Bultman Student Center is a game-changer for Hope. Our student activities and programs have a longstanding tradition of excellence, and now they have a home of their own. We want you to be a part of this exciting experience!

  • Inspired to lead? Develop leadership, responsibility and character through Student Congress, Student Activities Council, and eight fraternities and eight sororities.
  • Eager to expand worldviews? Gain and share new perspectives through Black Student Union, Latino Student Organization, Hope’s Asian Perspective Association and Theta Gamma Pi.
  • Wanting to worship? Make a joyful noise with Sacred Dance, Intervarsity Christian Fellowship or Hope Catholics.
  • Champion for a cause? Check out Green Hope, Colleges Against Cancer and Hope United for Justice.

These are just a few of the ways you can get involved as a Hope student. There are many other opportunities and places for you to find your passion, chart your way, and feel at home.

Hope’s community and beautiful campus are ready for you to do great things in and out of the classroom. Secure your spot at Hope today!
