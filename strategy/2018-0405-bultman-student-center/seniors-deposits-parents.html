Dear ${Contacts.Preferred First Name},

Last fall, the brand-new Bultman Student Center opened to much anticipation in the heart of our campus, and our students are now filling its space, using this beautiful facility at every opportunity.

This fall, your student, too, will enjoy all this 42,000 square-foot student-space has to offer at Hope: a living-room-like lounge featuring a fireplace and expansive views of our Pine Grove, a cafe with study spaces, a prayer chapel, a theater and a large open activity room for events.

The Bultman Student Center is a game-changer for Hope. Our student activities and programs have a longstanding tradition of excellence, and now they have a home of their own. Your student’s choices here are many.

  • Inspired to lead? Develop leadership, responsibility and character through Student Congress, Student Activities Council, and eight fraternities and eight sororities.
  • Eager to expand worldviews? Gain and share new perspectives through Black Student Union, Latino Student Organization, Hope’s Asian Perspective Association and Theta Gamma Pi.
  • Wanting to worship? Make a joyful noise with Sacred Dance, Intervarsity Christian Fellowship or Hope Catholics.
  • Champion for a cause? Check out Green Hope, Colleges Against Cancer and Hope United for Justice.

These are just a few of the ways your student can get involved at Hope. There are many other opportunities and places to find your passion, chart your way, and feel at home.

Hope’s dynamic community and beautiful campus are ready for your student to make great things happen in and out of the classroom. We look forward to having your student with us soon!

Sincerely,
Richard Frost
Vice President for Student Development and Dean of Students
