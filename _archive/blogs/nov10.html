If you didn't know it already, there are nine current Hope students blogging and tweeting away about their lives on Hope's campus. You can read all of their updates at: life.hope.edu. Each month we'll be bringing you some of their highlights in a special email. Here's what they've been posting about recently:
Brooke McDonald '13 shares about life around Hope in her Newsflash. Lauren Clack '11 has a great photo recap from her recent Fall Break trip to Portland, Oregon. Colleen Quick '14 writes about the Homecoming Hoedown, a unique event at Hope each Homecoming Weekend.
It's Halloween decoration time for Julie Anne Gruenhagen '11. CJ Newendorp '12 posts about the first Club Durf, one of the many dance events on campus each year. Jake Bajema '11 has some footage from this year's Pull. Have you ever seen anything like it?
Andi Springett '11 brings us another Snippet from the SUD (student union desk). This one's about Fall Break. Caleb Nykamp '12 gives us a great Homecoming recap. It's all about chemistry research for Xisen Hou '12.
