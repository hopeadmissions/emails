Check out Hope Sailing at Strictly Sail!

The Hope College Sailing Team will have a booth (#588) at Strictly Sail and we would love to see you there! Visit us Friday, January 25 through Sunday, January 27. Learn about collegiate sailing, what is new at Hope College, and support the team by purchasing a t-shirt.

Hope College Sailing is a club sport that provides a fun environment for sailors of all skill levels to learn the basics of sailing and small boat racing. Practices and home regattas are held at the Macatawa Bay Yacht Club in Holland, Michigan. Hope Sailing is a member of the Midwest Collegiate Sailing Association and the Inter-Collegiate Sailing Association.

Strictly Sail is the Midwest’s only and the nation’s largest indoor "All-Sail" boat show. It takes place January 24–27, 2013 at Chicago's famous Navy Pier. Strictly Sail has become a must-attend event for all sailors and has become one of Chicago’s favorite boat shows.
