Brochures and emails are great, but they just don't tell the whole story!

Come join us for one of our Visit Days this year. We offer these programs on most Fridays and some Mondays throughout the academic year, and they are great opportunity to experience all that Hope can offer you. You decide your schedule, and options include: a campus tour, a Chapel service, lunch in the dining hall, attending classes, academic information sessions, and more. Online registration is available.

Or, maybe you'd prefer to visit on another weekday. In that case, an individual visit would be just right for you. We'll customize a schedule for you that will include activities chosen by you, including: a campus tour, lunch in the dining hall, a Chapel service, meeting with a professor and/or coach, or even attending class! You can select your activities and register for your visit on our website. Online registration is available.

What's it like to be a student at Hope? Stay overnight in one of our residence halls and find out! Our student hosts will help you get a good sense of what student life is like at Hope. Please call (800) 968-7850 to register for an overnight visit.

Can't make it to campus on a weekday? On Saturday mornings Hope offers campus tours and the opportunity to meet with an admissions representative. (Saturday visits are by appointment only.) Please call (800) 968-7850 to register for a Saturday morning visit.
