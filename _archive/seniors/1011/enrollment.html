Hope College: Your Enrollment Deposit

How much?
$300

Is there really a deadline?
May 1 is the National Candidates' Reply Date, which assures you that if your $300 tuition deposit is received in our office by Sunday, May 1, your spot is guaranteed in next year's freshman class. Students whose deposits arrive after May 1 will secure a space as long as spaces are available. The College reserves the right to form a Wait List after May 1 should the class become full.

What are the options for paying the deposit?

  • Credit or debit card
  • Check or money order made out to "Hope College" and mailed to:
