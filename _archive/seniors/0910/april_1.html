Your Enrollment Deposit

How much?
$300

Is there really a deadline?
May 1 is the National Candidates' Reply Date, which assures you that if your $300 tuition deposit is received in our office by noon on Saturday, May 1, your spot is guaranteed in next year's freshman class. Students whose deposits arrive after noon on May 1 will secure a space as long as spaces are available. The College reserves the right to form a Wait List after May 1 should the class become full.

What are the options for paying the deposit?

  • Credit card
  • Check or money order made out to "Hope College" and mailed to:

    • Office of Admissions
    Hope College
    P.O. Box 9000
    Holland, Michigan 49422-9000

  • Wire transfer

What if I don’t have my financial aid award?
