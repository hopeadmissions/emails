Congratulations on being named a National Merit Semi-Finalist!
You have demonstrated an enviable record of academic success, and you are likely to have many college opportunities available to you. There are significant scholarships available to National Merit Scholars who list Hope as their first choice college. What is the Hope College National Merit Scholarship?
This four-year award provides a tuition scholarship in the amount of $17,000 per year; $68,000 over your entire Hope College experience! Hope will supplement any sponsorship by the National Merit Scholarship Corporation or a participating business corporation so that your total National Merit Scholarship equals $17,000 per year.
