Monday is March 1!

Whether you have filed or plan to file for financial assistance, please remember that preferred filing status goes to families who file the FAFSA by March 1, 2010. Due to the economic conditions in the state of Michigan we advise meeting this deadline, even if using estimated tax data for 2009 is necessary for completing the form.

Also, please note that Hope's Supplemental Application Form for Financial Aid (SAF) is required by March 1 as well.

Questions? Please contact us at (800) 968-7850 or admissions@hope.edu. You may also contact your Hope admissions representative for assistance with any specific questions you might have.
