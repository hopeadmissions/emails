In addition to new facilities, did you know...

  • Hope ranks 36th for the number of graduates who obtain a PhD in science, technology, engineering and mathematics.
  • Hope's chapter of Engineers Without Borders travels annually to Cameroon to assist with water purification projects.
  • 100% of the department's graduates find employment or are admitted to graduate programs.
  • The new facilities include more room for Hope's hallmark research collaboration between faculty and students, and space specifically for seniors to design and build their own projects or work on Hope's Formula SAE car for international competition.
  • Recent graduates now work at such places as Ford, General Motors, Honda, Lockheed Martin, Magna International, and Orbital Sciences Corporation.
