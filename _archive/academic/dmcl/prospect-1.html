Experience the World!
  • Choose from nearly 200 international study experiences in over 60 countries.
  • Travel one or two semesters during the academic year, or join one of the many 4-8 week summer programs.
  • Take classes in the local language or in English. Stay in universities or with host families.
Learn the Language
  • Major in Classical Languages (Latin and Greek), French, German, Japanese, or Spanish. Obtain a minor or concentration in Arabic, Mandarin, or Russian.
  • Learn from professors who are native speakers.
  • Travel to regions that use these languages. Not only is it encouraged, any financial aid or scholarship you receive travels with you.
Value the Major
  • Set yourself apart! Only 4% of US undergraduates study abroad. American companies are increasingly seeking employees who understand other cultures and can communicate effectively with them.
  • A language major is easily combined with many other majors. Popular combinations at Hope include Management, Political Science, History, International Studies, Nursing, Biology, Chemistry, Dance, Pre-Med, Psychology, Communications, and Exercise Science.
