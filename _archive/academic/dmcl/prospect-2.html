See the Future

Our majors are working in all types of national and international businesses and governmental agencies, hospitals, teaching (kindergarten through high school) and conducting academic research. They say learning another language gave them a definite advantage in the job market and applying to graduate programs by enhancing their understanding of other cultures and ways of thinking.

Earn Credit

Hope recognizes your previous study and experiences. Many students start at a level that gets them well on the way to a major after just one semester. Students who have studied to the AP or advanced level and have traveled are often able to gain two years of language credit after just one course!
