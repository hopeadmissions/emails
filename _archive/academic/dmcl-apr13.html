We are excited about your interest in Hope College and our language programs. We know this is "decision time" for you, so want to remind you of the reasons Hope is a great place to become proficient in the language(s) you love. Did you know…

  • Hope offers instruction in ten languages including Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Latin, Greek, Arabic, Chinese, Dutch and Russian.
  • The majority of Hope language professors are native speakers who bring you the inflection and cultural perspective necessary to become truly fluent.
  • Upper-level classes are limited to 15 students, igniting opportunities to use what you learn.
  • Professors are available (and love to!) work with you outside of class.
  • Hope offers 200 study-abroad programs all over the world to immerse you in language and culture.
  • Hope's language houses give you an immersion experience right on campus.

As the language philosopher Wittgenstein once said, "The limits of our language are the limits of our universe." My colleagues and I are passionate about our language programs and hope you will join our exciting quest to discover the world through language.
