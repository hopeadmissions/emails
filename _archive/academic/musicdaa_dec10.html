Students from all over the country come to Hope for many reasons- here are a few:

  • The student centered programming is at the heart of the Music Department’s many degree offerings (B.M. in Performance, B.M. in Jazz Performance, B.A. in Music, B.M. in Music Education, Music Composites, Minor in Music and Minor in Jazz Studies).

  • Every course in the Music Department is taught by one of Hope’s exceptional forty-plus member faculty.

  • The Department offers a multitude of ways to experience music - twenty performing ensembles, digital recording studios, world music exploration, internationally renowned guest artist residencies, and much, much more.

  • All Hope music students have the opportunity to explore international and domestic exchanges, classes, internships and tours.

Whether you wish to pursue a degree in music or simply wish to continue playing or learning about music - the Hope College Music Department has a place for you.
