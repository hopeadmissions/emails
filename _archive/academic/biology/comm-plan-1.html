Learn by Doing.

Why research? Hope professors involve biology students in collaborative research because they believe students best learn biology by doing biology. Our 11 biology professors, each holding a Ph.D. in their field, cover the breadth of biology, from ecological to molecular.

Read more about biology research at Hope »

What's more, unique opportunities to study biology in distant lands are available during Hope's May Term. Last year the Field Studies in Biology class took students to Peru and out on the Amazon River. This year the class will venture out on an expedition to the Galapagos Islands and Ecuador.

Read more about Field Studies in Biology »
