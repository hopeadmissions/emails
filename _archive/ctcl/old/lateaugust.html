The schools highlighted in the book Colleges That Change Lives by Loren Pope will be hosting an information session and college fair in your area soon. This is a great opportunity to meet admissions representatives from these schools. The program begins with a 30-minute presentation; the college fair follows immediately afterwards.

During the months of August and September, programs will be held in the following areas:

Minneapolis/St. Paul, MN — August 27, 2011
Chicago, IL — August 28, 2011
Columbus, OH — August 29, 2011
Indianapolis, IN — August 30, 2011
Kansas City, MO — August 31, 2011
Albuquerque, NM — September 1, 2011

These programs are free of charge and pre-registration is not required.
