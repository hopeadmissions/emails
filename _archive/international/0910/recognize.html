Does a college or university have my major? That is just the first question!

Be sure to also learn:

1. What is the student to teacher ratio?
2. Who teaches the undergraduate courses; Grad students or PhD professors?
3. What opportunities are there to work with professors on their research?
4. What are the department's placement rates into graduate and professional schools?

Fact: Placement rates into Masters and PhD programs are much higher from
America's elite liberal arts colleges like Hope than "big name" state universities.
