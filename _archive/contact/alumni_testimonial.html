Why I Help

I have enjoyed participating in the Alumni Calling Program the past few years because it gives me a chance to give back to Hope in a way that doesn’t require a financial donation, requires only a little bit of my time and is meaningful to prospective students.  I enjoy hearing about the students' study plans, their campus visit and how they learned about Hope.  They appreciate hearing about my time at Hope and why I chose Hope. Sometimes I get questions I can’t answer, but I just refer them to their admissions rep and mention the questions in the Admissions feedback form.  The personal touch seems to make the students feel special and that someone cares about them and their college decision!
—Anita Bateman,‘98
