ALUMNI AND PARENT CONTACT PROGRAM AT HOPE COLLEGE

We're going live.

The Parent Contact Program is about to go live! Thank you so much for your willingness to participate and share your insights with parents of students considering Hope for this coming fall.

This email is to provide you a "sneak peek" of the site and to let you review your section before we make the site public.

A few things to know and remember…

  • You are listed in at least one of the categories named along the side of the landing page. We wanted to keep the groups at a reasonable length so did our best to place you in the most appropriate area(s) based on your responses.
  • Unfortunately, it is not feasible to tell each volunteer what categories they are in. To find your listing, we suggest using the "Find" function in your web browser (usually control-F or command-F).
  • Some volunteer responses were cut off by the survey instrument. We have done our best to finish your thoughts accurately, but if we have missed the mark, please let us know and we will make the change you suggest.
  • We will be sending an email to the parents of all admitted students around March 13. We have no idea how much email this will generate for you! We will monitor traffic to the various pages and will survey volunteers in May to learn how many emails they received.
  • If you have any questions or concerns about emails you receive, please contact me.

Lastly, and most importantly, thank you so much for your willingness to participate. Reading the comments and activities of our Hope students has been a gratifying experience and we know your insights will be of great interest to prospective families.
