Thank you again for agreeing to participate in this important effort! The names and contact information for the 3 families you have agreed to contact are below. Every effort was made to match you with students from your state. If you have students from another state it is because we have more volunteers than prospects from your state. Also, here are a few pointers to get you started:

All of these students have been accepted for admission to Hope College. However, please recognize that since the sending of this email, some students may have decided to attend another institution.

The purpose of your call is to talk with the student's parent and offer to answer any questions that he/she may have about Hope and what the Hope experience has been like for your family. We do not expect you to be an expert on the facts and figures of Hope College. Our admissions staff will follow up with any specific questions. To begin the call, we suggest you:

  1. Identify yourself as a Hope Parent.
  2. Indicate that the Hope Admissions Office has made you aware that their child is considering Hope.
  3. Offer to discuss any questions or give your experience with regards to Hope College.

After your conversation please provide your Conversation Details (one form for each admitted student.) This evaluation can serve as a guide for your conversation as well.

We would appreciate receiving this information back as soon as possible and no later than 7-10 days from your receipt of this email. The information you submit will be forwarded to the admissions counselor working with each student.

Please reply to this email with any questions. We appreciate your help in recruiting excellent students to Hope!

Carrie Olesh, Admissions Representative
