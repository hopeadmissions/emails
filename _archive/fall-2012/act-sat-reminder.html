The second half of your junior year has arrived, which means now is the time to begin thinking about an important piece of the college application process - the ACT and/or SAT. 

Here's what you need to know regarding taking these tests and Hope College:

  • Hope accepts either ACT or SAT for both admission and merit scholarship consideration.
  • To ensure Hope receives your results, use the appropriate code when registering.
    • Hope Codes: ACT: 2012 / SAT: 1301
  • For merit scholarships Hope uses your best individual test.
  • Hope does not require the writing component of either test.

Go to ACT test and SAT test for registration information, test dates, and lots of helpful information. Don't delay as registration deadlines are well ahead of test dates.

Please write us if you have any questions. We look forward to receiving your results!
