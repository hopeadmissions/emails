The George F. Baker Scholars Program.

Hope College is one of only 33 colleges & universities nationwide to have been granted membership into the prestigious George F. Baker Scholars Program for business. The Baker Scholars are a student-led organization and are advised by Hope faculty. Through off-campus trips to corporate offices, meetings with national business leaders, and participating in social events and service, the Baker Scholars graduate prepared for a successful career in business and in life.
