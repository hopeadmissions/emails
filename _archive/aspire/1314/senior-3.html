Seasons of Change.

As spring bursts forth in Holland, Hope students anticipate graduation, May Term, summer research projects, and bittersweet goodbyes as some return home for the summer.

Graduation is the height of academic splendor and ceremony and an explosion of celebration afterward.

Hope’s May Term offers students yet another time for on- or off-campus experiences, from communication study in Scotland to biology in New Zealand to education on the Rosebud Indian Reservation in South Dakota and so many other opportunities around the world.

More about May Term →

Student researchers abound on campus thanks to SHARP, the Summer Hope Academic Research Program. This year Hope was ranked #1 nationally among all liberal arts colleges for research grants received from the Research Corporation for Science Advancement (RCSA). Hope has been awarded 64 grants throughout the foundation’s 100-year history.

More about SHARP →

As you anticipate your own graduation, you may experience that bittersweet feeling, leaving home to follow your path to Hope. You can look forward to Orientation and a whole new class of friends to get to know.
