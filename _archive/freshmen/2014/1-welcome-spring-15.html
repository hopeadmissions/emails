Beginning your future at Hope College  
Welcome to Hope!

We look forward to seeing you for the Spring 2015 semester. Let us introduce you to some of our offices.

Division of Student Development

Student Development is headed by the Dean of Students, Richard Frost, and is committed to engaging each student. Your energy is what defines Hope and fuels our community. We try to capture this energy in all the events we plan and clubs in which you participate. We coordinate your housing assignments and help create a safe environment. Ultimately, we believe your journey at Hope will lead you through intellectual discernment, deeper understanding of the Christian faith, and personal growth.

Office of the Registrar

The Registrar’s Office is led by Carol De Jong and responsible for maintaining your educational record and coordinating academic advising and registration. We will assist you in creating your class schedule and make sure you have the resources to stay on track for graduation. We will report your grades, process your transcripts, and help you make changes to your schedule. In short, we are here to help guide in your academic side of your college career.

Business Services

Business Services is directed by Doug VanDyken and supports you in your financial planning and payments. We help you understand the charges and credits that have been added to your account. We can assist you in setting up online payments, enrolling in payment plans, and will send you notifications for pending charges on your bill.

Please make sure the following items are completed before you arrive at Hope in January. The college is closed from December 24 until January 5. You may have taken care of some or all of these already – thank you! If not, let's go!

Registration

Registration is done individually. If you have not registered please reach out to the Registrar's office to set up a plan to do so. Email registrar@hope.edu or call 616.395.7760.

Orientation and Housing

Contact Student Development for further information regarding the dates for Spring Orientation and questions about housing. Email sdev@hope.edu or call 616.395.7800.

Payment

KnowHope Plus account is the portal for gaining access to bill payment, account statements, and financial aid information.

To begin the Bill Payment process:

  • Log into KnowHope Plus
  • Choose Registrar and Student Accounts
  • Choose Student Accounts
  • Choose Online Bill and Payment
  • Create “Parent PINs” (If you have parents, guardians, grandparents, etc., helping you with your bill, you will need to give them access to pay your bill.)
