As you prepare for the transition to Hope College, we invite you to add to your first year experience by participating in the Phelps Scholars Program!

The program offers the opportunity to live and learn with students from a variety of backgrounds as well as free cultural field trips, meaningful conversations and community-building activities. While participation requires a one-year commitment, it is our goal for all Phelps Scholars to get off to a great start, learn about the world and its people, succeed in college and in life and have fun along the way.
