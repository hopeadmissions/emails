Beginning your future at Hope College  
Just a few more items for your cart.

The beginning of the summer came slowly and now the end is fast approaching. Thank you for getting many tasks completed to prepare you for being here at Hope. There are a few items left to complete and your cart will be ready for checkout!

Take time to check out the Orientation website – full of great hints as you finish your lists and fill up your dorm room shopping cart. Oh, and don’t forget to register for Orientation if you haven’t already!

  • NCAA Division III Hope fall athletes: read the email titled “Important Information Regarding Athletic Eligibility.”
  • Complete your student loan requirements. Students who have been awarded a Federal Perkins and/or Federal Direct Loan must complete an online Master Promissory Note and the entrance counseling requirements. These steps must be completed to officially accept your student loan(s):
    • For Federal Direct Loans, go to studentloans.gov.
    • For Federal Perkins Loans, go to signmyloan.com.
  • Begin the Bill Payment process:
    1. Log into KnowHope Plus
    2. Choose Registrar and Student Accounts
    3. Choose Student Accounts
    4. Choose Online Bill and Payment
    5. Create "Parent PINs" (If you have parents, guardians, grandparents, etc., helping you with your bill, you will need to give them access to pay your bill.)
  • Contact the Orientation Office at orientation@hope.edu or 616.395.7882 if you have not received your Orientation booklet in the mail.
