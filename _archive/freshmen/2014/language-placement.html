If you are a native or near-native speaker of Spanish (if you speak Spanish at home, for example) you should take Spanish 321-01 or 322-41. Spanish 321-01 is designed specifically for Spanish speakers with oral proficiency but little or no formal training in the language. Spanish 322-41 is a review of grammar and is intended for students who are native speakers and have had formal training (are able to read & write in Spanish). Please contact Professor Liliana Dorado (dorado@hope.edu) with questions about either of these courses.
