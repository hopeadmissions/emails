Congratulations are in order. Some of you have recently graduated from high school and some of you are about to do so – way to go! We realize it's a busy time for you.

Future Let's Go emails will be sent every two weeks and will have important items that need to be completed in order to prepare you for being at Hope College. We cannot stress enough the importance of taking care of these items in order to make your first memories and moments at Hope great! So, please continue to check your Hope College email.

Oh, and by the way, if you have not signed up for Hope on Facebook, now is the time! The Orientation staff will be posting there often this summer. If you have questions, please connect with the offices we introduced to you in the first email – they want to meet you. So, let's go! You have classes to register for and other items to take care of.

  • Read the latest Tuesday Tiding email: this is in your Hope email and includes information to help you register for classes.
  • Resources for registration.
  • Attend New Student Advising Day on July 12: an optional half-day program for incoming first-year students.
  • Submit your Health Forms by Tuesday, July 1. These were mailed to you in the #hope2018 envelope.
  • Log into KnowHope Plus to familiarize yourself with the portal where all your class schedule information will appear (viewable August 1) and Hope College bills will be paid.
