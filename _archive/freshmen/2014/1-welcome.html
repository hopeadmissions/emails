Beginning your future at Hope College  
Welcome to Hope!

There are many people preparing for your arrival in August. In the coming weeks, however, we know you are busy preparing for high school graduation. Enjoy this wonderful moment in your life! As you wrap up your high school career we want you to start thinking about the enrollment process at Hope College.

So here is the plan: In the next couple of weeks you will receive a packet with #hope2018 on the outside. The contents of this envelope, which includes your new Hope College email account, will help you begin the process of joining the Hope community! Rather than sending one large packet, we will be sending incremental messages to your Hope email account throughout the summer. Watch your email closely and be sure to respond accordingly. Our goal is to build momentum and excitement as you anticipate joining the Hope community this fall!

Your parents, guardians and mentors might also be wondering what's next! Would you please share this plan with them? Parents and guardians will also be receiving an overview of the process and similar information around the same time you receive your #hope2018 envelope. Enjoy your graduation! We will continue to be in touch.
