We Live for This.

As you wrap up your junior year, you are preparing for college in so many ways — from keeping your GPA in good shape, to excelling in clubs and sports, and focusing on your college search.

In the fall of your senior year, it’s time to narrow your college choice. Savor your last, but challenging year in high school, making sure you choose the classes and student activities that best prepare you for college.

At Hope College, we encourage you to pursue what fulfills you with your unique gifts and interests. Our mission is that our students become fully alive — in mind, body and spirit.

Hope piques your curiosity and offers a place to take risks. Our students join organizations new to them, choose challenging subjects, play different sports, even study abroad with very different languages and cultures. Like you, they are preparing for the next chapter in their lives by taking full advantage of all the opportunities and experiences offered to them now.

At Hope College, we truly live for this. And we hope you will visit Hope and experience what can be yours in your college years.
