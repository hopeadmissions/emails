It is always exciting to hear where this important language is being offered to US high school students. We are also excited to introduce you to Hope College’s Mandarin Chinese program and encourage you to share it with your students who want to continue their Mandarin studies in college.

The Hope College Mandarin Chinese Minor is designed for students wishing to master Mandarin as well as develop their knowledge of Chinese politics, economy and history. Students have combined this minor with a wide variety of other Hope programs including Political Science, International Relations, Communication, English and Pre-Medicine. In addition to language instruction, students are able to choose from ten semester-long programs in Beijing, Shanghai, Nanjing, and Yunan.

Our small classes of eight to ten students allow for very individualized attention, and on the first day of class each student is paired with a native speaker for drills and homework assistance. Outside the classroom our students continue developing their language skills and cultural awareness through our Chinese Culture Cafe, student-organized celebrations of the Moon Festival, Lunar New Year Festival, food fairs, a Chinese Bible study, and our annual Images: A Reflection of Cultures event.

Hope College offers unrivaled opportunities for students to participate in faculty-student collaborative research, and dedicated faculty members often supervise advanced students in independent studies. Our Mellon Scholars Program is a good example. For students with three years of Chinese, these are valuable ways to develop their language proficiency further and in a unique direction.

We would be pleased to have you share this information with your students. We are also eager to be a resource to you. If you would like to discuss ways in which we can partner or opportunities at Hope for your students, please do reach out! 祝
