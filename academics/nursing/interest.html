On behalf of the Hope College Nursing program, we are happy to hear of your interest in both Hope College and our nursing program. Nursing is a wonderful career choice with limitless possibilities from traditional bedside patient care to administration, education, missions, and advanced degrees such as a nurse practitioner, nurse anesthetist, nurse midwife, or nurse researcher.

Hope College nursing graduates are well prepared for each of these career opportunities. Here’s why:

  • Our nursing students are prepared to care for patients where moral and ethical situations are present because of our classes being taught within a Christian context.
  • Nursing students learn to provide compassionate care for the health needs of culturally diverse people of all ages in a variety of settings.
  • Our senior nursing students complete an extensive nursing internship, working with an acute care nurse in their area of interest, which prepares them for their job as a Registered Nurse.
  • The nursing program at Hope prepares students well for Master's and Doctoral programs. A distinctive for our students is each of them participates in a research practicum prior to graduation.

We invite you to visit our website for student perspectives, course offerings and admissions guidelines.

We look forward to hearing from you!
