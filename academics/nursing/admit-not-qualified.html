Congratulations on your acceptance to Hope College! We are excited to learn of this decision and your continued interest in the field of nursing within our program.

As you weigh your options for your undergraduate education, please keep in mind that our students are well-prepared for successful completion of the licensure exam and for a smooth transition to graduate education or a career as a professional nurse.

  • You’ll apply to the nursing program during the spring semester of your freshman year (as long as you’ve taken the required prerequisite courses).
  • If accepted to the program, you’ll begin nursing courses in your sophomore year after completing the required prerequisite courses.
  • Learn more by visiting our website for student perspectives, course offerings and much more.

If you have not already done so, I encourage you to visit our campus and meet with faculty in our department to learn more about Hope College and our nursing program. I look forward to you potentially joining our nursing program.

We look forward to hearing from you!
