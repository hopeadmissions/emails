We’re glad that you are considering coming to Hope College! It's an exciting time of life! We want to let you know about an important scholarship opportunity available to you as a potential Hope student.

The Center for Ministry Studies at Hope College offers programs to encourage life-long Christian commitment through both local and global involvement in the body of Christ.

Have you ever considered doing some kind of ministry or work that serves the church in a multicultural or ethnically diverse setting? Maybe you're thinking of becoming a pastor, teacher, youth minister, social worker or musician in a community of this kind. If so, the CrossRoads Scholarship opportunity is for you!

Scholarship Amount

Varies, dependent on demonstrated financial need.

Number of Years

Renewable for four years.

Application

Three brief questions and one letter of recommendation to be sent as an email attachment. See the scholarship website for more information. More links follow this message.

Deadline

Tuesday, February 9, 2016, at midnight.

Whether or not you are interested in this specific opportunity, you may find other programs through the Center for Ministry Studies to be helpful to you while you’re at Hope. We’re looking forward to getting to know you.

Best wishes as you continue to consider your options for college. If we can do anything to help or if you have questions, please do not hesitate to contact us.
